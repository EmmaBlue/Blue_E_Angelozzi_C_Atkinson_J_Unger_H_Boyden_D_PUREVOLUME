Time Line

July 2012

Frank Ocean releases Channel Orange a week early.

December 2012

Gangnam Style becomes the 1st Youtube video to reach 1 billion views.

January 2013

Bastille releases Pompeii, which peaks at #2 on the UK Singles chart.

September 2013

Lorde’s Pure Heroine debuts at #3 on the US Billboard 200 chart.

March 2014

Robin Thicke releases Blurred Lines. It peaks at #1 in over 25 countries.

December 2014

Spotify names x the most streamed album in the world.

August 2015

Here by Alessia Cara debuts at #95 on Billboard’s Hot 100.

December 2015

Alabama Shakes’ Sound & Color is nominated for 6 Grammies.

July 2016

Drake names Where Did I Go by Jorja Smith his music pick.

October 2016

The music video for Closer by The Chainsmokers comes out.

June 2017

Brock Hampton releases Saturation I to critical acclaim.

July 2017

Cardi B becomes the 5th female rapper ever to top Billboard Hot 100.

March 2018

Superorganism releases its self-titled album a year after forming.
